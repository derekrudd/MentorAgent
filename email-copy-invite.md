## Notes for design / dev

- Match the reference's card-on-dark-background layout, rounded corners (~16px), generous padding.
- CTA button: dark fill, white text, full width inside the inset card.
- Body type: a clean sans-serif (Inter, Söhne, or similar) at ~16px with comfortable line-height.
- Headline weight: bold/semibold, ~24–28px.
- Personalization tokens: `{{first_name}}`, sign-in URL, notification preferences URL.
- Keep the email under ~600px wide for inbox rendering.
